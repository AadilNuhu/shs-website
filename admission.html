Welcome to Accra Academy!

We're thrilled that you're interested in joining our school community. Below, you'll find information on our admission procedures.

Admission Criteria

Our admission criteria include:

  • Academic requirements

    • Thank you for your interest in Accra Academy. Below are the general academic requirements for admission:

    • Minimum grades in English, Mathematics, and Science
    • Entrance exam score (if applicable)
    • Interview may be required
    • Specific course prerequisites
    • Additional criteria such as extracurricular activities or community service

    Please note that specific requirements may vary and it's best to contact the admissions office for the most accurate information.

  • Age eligibility

    • To be eligible for admission to Accra Academy, applicants must have completed their Junior High School education. We recognize the importance of a solid academic foundation, and our doors are open to students who are ready to embark on a transformative journey in senior high school.

Application Process

To apply to Accra Academy, follow these steps:

  1. Complete the online application form or obtain a physical application form from the admissions office.
  2. Submit the application form along with any required supporting documents.

Accra Academy Cut-off Points

Below are the cut-off points for admission to Accra Academy:

Program Cut-off Points
General Science 6-12
General Arts 8-15
Business 10-18
Visual Arts 6-14

Please note that these cut-off points are approximate and may vary from year to year.
